Executive Summary

All four countries are "thin markets" — too few investors, too few investable opportunities — resulting in a double-sided market failure.

Rather than replicating existing accelerator and training programs, CAF should focus its resources on structural market formation: unlocking institutional capital, building exit infrastructure, and creating the conditions where entrepreneurship becomes a rational outcome.
