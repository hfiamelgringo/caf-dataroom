# Small Ideas

Capacity Building and Technical Assistance: Support governments and regulators in building institutional capacity for venture ecosystem oversight and development, including training programs, policy workshops, and peer learning networks.

## Venture Capital Fund Licensing Framework

Establish a simplified licensing framework for small venture capital funds (USD 5-25 million) that reduces regulatory burden while maintaining investor protections. Many early-stage fund managers are deterred from formalizing their operations due to complex licensing requirements. A tiered licensing framework could accelerate fund creation and formalization in the region.

## Startup Tax Credit Program

Create targeted tax credits for individuals and corporate investors who deploy capital into early-stage startups. Tax credits could be structured to incentivize investment in specific sectors (e.g., fintech, agritech) or geographic regions (e.g., non-capital-city locations). Tax credits have proven effective in other emerging markets for stimulating venture investment without direct government expenditure.
