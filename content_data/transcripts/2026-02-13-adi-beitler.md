- Recommendations for CAF Impact Fund Design
- Critical first decision: debt vs equity fund structure
- For equity fund success, requires three key elements
- Honest conversation about adjusted multiple expectations (2x-3x acceptable vs traditional VC expectations)
- Patient capital with longer exit timelines (year 7+ acceptable)
- Strong governance structures protecting mission
- 80% quorum required to change company mission (vs standard 51%)
- Founders retain key decision-making power
- Preferred vs ordinary share structures that don’t prioritize investor returns
