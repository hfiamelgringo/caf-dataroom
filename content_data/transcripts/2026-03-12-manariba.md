## Context
Manariba is a microbiologist and co-founder of LiSalud, a Honduran SaaS startup offering cloud-based management systems for clinical laboratories, with 80+ clients in Honduras and early expansion into El Salvador. This interview captures her firsthand account of building a healthtech company in one of the region's most challenging startup ecosystems, including financing gaps, trust barriers in B2B health markets abroad, and concrete policy recommendations for supporting digital startups in Honduras.
