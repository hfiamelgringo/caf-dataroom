- Legal certainty - clear rules like Mexico/Brazil fintech laws

- UBO disclosure flexibility - funds reluctant to share LP details with local governments

- Ultimate Beneficial Ownership requirements discourage fund investment

- Tax treaties more valuable than local fund structures (example: Mexico-Canada treaty)
- Employee stock options: Less regulation better - most LatAm countries allow 1¢ grants vs US fair market value requirements
- Brazil most advanced market with legal certainty and transparency
- Chile/Mexico mixed results:
- Chile’s Corfo program successful for early funding but creates exit barriers
- Standardization across 28 LatAm jurisdictions needed but challenging
