## Context
This interview captures a multi-stakeholder conversation facilitated through Mike Putnam, bringing together Barnaby Dorfman (MainSail Partners PE, former Amazon/PayScale), Mark Lopez (Partnership for the Americas, former IDB), Geoffrey Kirkman (former World Bank/Kennedy School), and Jonathan Nelson (Hackers and Founders) to explore replicating a B2B SaaS acquisition and growth model in Latin America. The discussion is directly relevant to CAF's innovation strategy given PFA's pending CAF proposal and the group's focus on underserved smaller markets (Costa Rica, Honduras, Guatemala, Dominican Republic) where capital gaps and AI leapfrog opportunities are pronounced.
