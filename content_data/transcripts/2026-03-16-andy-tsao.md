- Latin America Banking Services
- Started 15 years ago when LatAm ecosystem was tiny
- One of few banks focusing exclusively on innovation economy
- Services for companies operating in US dollars:

- Critical mass drops steeply beyond these countries

- Venture debt: ~30% of equity raised can be borrowed
- Cheaper than equity dilution for founders
- Requires pattern recognition and VC relationship expertise
- Latin Growth Lending Fund partnership with IDB Invest and Partners for Growth
- Still operating, providing venture debt in region

- Capital call financing lines

- Multi-country operations complicate loan documentation costs
- Traditional banking products don’t fit startup loss profiles
- Market has more competition now vs. 15 years ago
- LatAm ecosystem growth modest in recent years
- Open to partnering on new financing schemes with development agencies
